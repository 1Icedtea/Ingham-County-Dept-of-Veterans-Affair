We show Honor To those who served

Benefits

As a Veteran you are entitled to various benefits including Property Tax Exemption, VA compensation, VA Disability Pension, Home Loan Guaranty, Small Business and Employment Resources, Education.

VA health care

VA health care is available for local veterans. For assistance in applying for VA Health Care please contact Ingham County Department of Veteran Affairs at (517) 887-4331.

Survivors Memorial

There are several benefits that commemorate a veteran's sacrifice to our nation and benefits for survivors. It is our privilege to discuss available resources with you and your loved ones.
